Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 6, 2025

Last Updated: August 6, 2025

Privacy-First Promise: Yappr is built with privacy as our core principle. We don't collect, store, or transmit your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Don't Collect

Unlike many applications, Yappr is designed to not collect any personal information:

  • ❌ No user accounts or registration required
  • ❌ No personal identifiers collected
  • ❌ No usage analytics or telemetry
  • ❌ No tracking cookies or persistent identifiers
  • ❌ No transcription data stored on our servers
  • ❌ No voice recordings stored anywhere

2. How Your Data is Handled

Local Storage Only

All your data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API:

  • ✅ API keys stored in Chrome's secure storage
  • ✅ Transcription history saved locally on your device
  • ✅ Usage statistics kept on your machine
  • ✅ Preferences and settings stored locally

Third-Party Service Usage

When you use Yappr's features, your data may be sent to these services you choose:

ElevenLabs (Speech-to-Text):

  • Your voice audio is sent directly to ElevenLabs for transcription
  • Audio is processed and deleted according to ElevenLabs' privacy policy
  • We do not store or have access to your voice recordings
  • Review: ElevenLabs Privacy Policy

OpenAI (AI Enhancement - Optional):

  • If you use AI enhancement presets, your transcribed text is sent to OpenAI
  • Only the text transcript is sent, never your voice
  • Processing is done according to OpenAI's privacy policy
  • Review: OpenAI Privacy Policy

3. Data Control & Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • View Your Data: All data is visible in the extension's interface
  • Export Data: Download your transcription history anytime
  • Delete Data: Clear all history and data through the extension
  • Uninstall: Removing the extension deletes all local data

4. API Keys and Security

Your API keys are handled securely:

  • Stored using Chrome's secure storage mechanism
  • Never transmitted to Yappr servers (we don't have any!)
  • Only sent to the official API endpoints you configure
  • Encrypted in transit using HTTPS

5. Permissions Explanation

Yappr requests these Chrome permissions for legitimate functionality:

  • activeTab: Insert transcribed text into the current webpage
  • storage: Save your preferences and transcription history locally
  • scripting: Enable keyboard shortcuts for recording
  • notifications: Show success/error messages
  • tabs: Open help and settings pages

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date above
  • Major changes will be highlighted in extension updates
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
